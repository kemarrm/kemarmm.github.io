My hero is My Uncle Kevin Williams. As a software engineer, my uncle has always been someone I admire. He inspires me to stay focused and work hard in school, just as he did when he was pursuing his own studies. Watching him navigate his career with dedication and a passion for technology has shown me the value of being organized and diligent His career has also shaped my own ambitions.
